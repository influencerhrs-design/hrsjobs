Platform FAQ

How do I apply for a role?
Click the 'Apply Now' button on any job listing. It will prompt you to send an email with your credentials directly to our hiring team.
Is there a cost for candidates?
No. HRS is a free platform for creators and job seekers to browse and apply for opportunities.
How can my company post a job?
Brands and agencies can contact us at hrs.team.a@gmail.com to get their roles vetted and listed on our dashboard.
What niches do you cover?
We specialize in the creative economy, including Fashion, Lifestyle, Digital Assets, Tech, and Social Media Marketing.
